over quiet dream cities
everyday
we’d sit in the park

GREAT HILL NY, NY
we'd sleep under layers
tomorrow, it'd be 67°
okay. pure.

space
time
The Window
Air

A Mirror
magnetised to life
hieroglyphs of flesh
breaths lay, living

         
a smile
Arc
a laugh
a body, broken
dancing

        
TALL MEN WALTZED
I, off to the side
watched at shoulder-level
you were dancing
could you see me?
